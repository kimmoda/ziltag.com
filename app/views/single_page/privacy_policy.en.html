Privacy Policy September, 2015

There are many different ways you can use our services – to watch videos and view photos, to communicate with other people, to buy commodities, to obtain information, or to create new content. The information you share with us, for example when creating a Ziltag Account, can help us make those services better. As you use our services, we would like you to be well informed on how we are using the information and various measures in which you can protect your privacy.

Our Privacy Policy explains:

  • What type of information we collect and why we collect it.
  • How we use that information.
  • The choices we offer, including how to access and update information.
  • Rights derived from your personal information.

We have tried to keep it as simple as possible, but if you are not familiar with terms like cookies, IP addresses, pixel tags and browsers, please read the definition of these key terms first. Your privacy matters to Ziltag so whether you are new to Ziltag or a long-time user, please do take the time to get to know our practices – and contact us at our customer service line or email us at service@ziltag.com if you have any questions.

Information we collect

We collect information to provide better services to all of our users – from figuring out basic stuff like which language you use, to more complex things such as which ads you will find most useful, preferences and rewards.

We collect information in two ways:

  • Information you give us. For example, many of our services require you to sign up for a Ziltag Account. When you do, we may ask for personal information, such as your name, email address, date of birth, gender, telephone number or credit card number.
  • Information we collect from your use of our services. We may collect information about the services that you use and how you use them, such as when you watch videos on Ziltag or when you view our ads and interact with the content. This information may include:

    • Device information

      We may collect device-specific information (such as your hardware model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information including phone number). Ziltag may associate your device identifiers or phone number with your Ziltag Account.

    • Log information

      When you use our services or view content provided by Ziltag, we may automatically collect and store certain information in server logs. This may include:

      • details of how you have used our services, such as your search queries.
      • telephone log information like your phone number, number of incoming calls, forwarding numbers, time and date of calls, duration of calls, SMS routing information and types of calls.
      • Internet protocol address.
      • device event information such as crashes, system activity, hardware settings, browser type, browser language, the date and time of your request and referral URL.
      • cookies that may uniquely identify your browser or your Ziltag Account.

    • Location information

      When you use a location-enabled Ziltag service, we may collect and process information about your actual location, like GPS signals sent by a mobile device. We may also use various technologies to determine your location, such as sensor data from your device that may, for example, provide information on nearby Wi-Fi access points and cell towers.

    • Unique application numbers

      Certain services include a unique application number. This number and information about your installation (for example, the operating system type and application version number) may be sent to Ziltag when you install or uninstall that service or when that service periodically connect with our servers, such as for automatic updates.

    • Local storage

      We may collect and store information (including personal information) locally on your device using mechanisms such as browser web storage (including HTML 5) and application data caches.

    • Cookies and anonymous identifiers

      We use various technologies to collect and store information when you visit a Ziltag service, and this may include sending one or more cookies or anonymous identifiers to your device. We also use cookies and anonymous identifiers when you interact with services we offer to our partners, such as advertising services or Ziltag features that may appear on other sites.

How we use information we collect

We will collect, process, and use your personal information based on contracts, or similar obligations under contracts, administrative management, information provision, statistic investigation and analysis, and other specified purposes within the scope of our business.

We use the information we collect from all of our services to provide, maintain, protect and improve them, to develop new ones, and to protect Ziltag and our users. We also use this information to offer you tailored content – such as giving you more relevant search results and ads.

We may use the name you provide to Ziltag across all of the services we offer that require a Ziltag Account. In addition, we may replace past names associated with your Ziltag Account so that you are represented consistently across all our services.

When you contact Ziltag, we may keep a record of your communication to help solve any issues you might be facing. We may use your email address to inform you about our services, such as letting you know about upcoming changes or improvements.

We use information collected from cookies and other technologies, such as pixel tags, to improve your user experience and the overall quality of our services. For example, by saving your language preferences, we will be able to have our services appear in the language you prefer. When showing you tailored ads, we will not associate a cookie or anonymous identifier with sensitive categories, such as those based on race, religion, sexual orientation or health.

We may combine personal information from one service with information, including personal information, from other Ziltag services for specific purpose – for example to make it easier to share things with people you know. We will not combine DoubleClick cookie information with personally identifiable information unless we have your opt-in consent.

We will ask for your consent before using your information for a purpose other than those that are set out in this Privacy Policy.

Ziltag processes personal information on our servers in many countries around the world. We may process your personal information on a server located outside the country/region where you live.

Transparency and choice

People have different privacy concerns. Our goal is to be clear about what information we collect, so that you can make meaningful choices about how it is used.

You may also set your browser to block all cookies, including cookies associated with our services, or to indicate when a cookie is being set by us. However, it is important to remember that many of our services may not function properly if your cookies are disabled. For example, we may not remember your language preferences.

Information you share

Many of our services let you share information with others. Remember that when you share information publicly, it may be indexable by search engines. Our services provide you with different options on sharing and removing your content.
